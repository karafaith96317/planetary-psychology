# Research Roadmap

## Phase 1: Define the Framework

Create clear definitions, boundaries, and terminology for Planetary Psychology™.

## Phase 2: Identify Measurable Variables

Human variables:
- Mood
- Sleep
- Stress
- Energy
- Attention
- Creativity

Environmental variables:
- AQI
- Temperature
- Humidity
- Barometric pressure
- Light exposure

Elemental variables:
- Water minerals
- Soil composition
- Atmospheric particulates
- Nutritional minerals

Planetary variables:
- Lunar phase
- Solar activity
- Geomagnetic activity
- Seasonal cycles

## Phase 3: Build Small Studies

Start with personal and public datasets.

## Phase 4: Expand to Collaboration

Invite researchers, developers, psychologists, environmental scientists, and data analysts.

## Phase 5: Publish Findings

Document hypotheses, methods, datasets, limitations, and results.
