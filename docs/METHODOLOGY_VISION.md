# Planetary Psychology™ Methodology Vision

## Purpose

Planetary Psychology™ proposes that human psychological and behavioral states emerge within nested biological, environmental, geographical, elemental, temporal, and planetary systems.

The purpose of this methodology is to provide a framework for studying these relationships using measurable variables and reproducible methods.

---

# Core Principle

Human beings do not exist in isolation.

Psychological states emerge through interactions among:

- Biological systems
- Environmental systems
- Social systems
- Elemental systems
- Planetary systems
- Temporal systems

Planetary Psychology™ seeks to investigate these interactions through data-driven research.

---

# Layered Research Model

## Layer 1: Individual Biology

Variables may include:

- Age
- Sex
- Height
- Weight
- Blood pressure
- Resting heart rate
- Heart-rate variability (HRV)
- Sleep duration
- Physical activity

---

## Layer 2: Psychology

Variables may include:

- Mood
- Stress
- Anxiety
- Depression
- Personality measures
- Cognitive performance
- Resilience
- Social connectedness

---

## Layer 3: Behavior

Variables may include:

- Diet
- Exercise
- Screen time
- Substance use
- Work patterns
- Social interaction

---

## Layer 4: Geography

Variables may include:

- Latitude
- Longitude
- Elevation
- Urban/rural classification
- Distance from water
- Population density

---

## Layer 5: Environment

Variables may include:

- AQI
- Temperature
- Humidity
- Atmospheric pressure
- Light pollution
- Noise exposure

---

## Layer 6: Elemental Systems

Variables may include:

- Water mineral content
- Soil chemistry
- Trace metals
- Nutritional intake
- Environmental chemistry

---

## Layer 7: Planetary Systems

Variables may include:

- Lunar phase
- Solar activity
- Geomagnetic activity
- Magnetic declination
- Seasonal cycles
- Day length

---

## Layer 8: Temporal Systems

Variables may include:

- Time zone
- Circadian timing
- Local solar time
- Sleep timing
- Seasonal timing

---

# Research Philosophy

Planetary Psychology™ encourages:

- Open inquiry
- Data transparency
- Statistical rigor
- Reproducibility
- Interdisciplinary collaboration

The framework does not assume causation.

Relationships should be tested empirically and revised as evidence accumulates.

---

# Long-Term Goal

To develop an integrated systems model capable of exploring how biological, environmental, elemental, geographical, temporal, and planetary variables interact with psychological and behavioral outcomes.
