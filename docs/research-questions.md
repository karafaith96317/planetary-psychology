# Research Questions

## Primary Question

How do biological, elemental, environmental, social, planetary, and temporal systems interact with human psychological states and collective behavior?

## Study Questions

1. Does air quality correlate with mood, sleep, stress, or cognitive performance?
2. Do mineral and elemental environments correlate with population-level health or psychological outcomes?
3. Do antipodal locations show measurable atmospheric or behavioral correlations?
4. Do lunar phase, solar activity, or geomagnetic activity correlate with human behavioral data?
5. Can environmental and planetary variables improve prediction of psychological state when combined with biological and social variables?
