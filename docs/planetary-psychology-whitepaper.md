## Abstract

Planetary Psychology‚ĄĘ is an interdisciplinary framework for studying how human psychology and collective behavior may be influenced by biological, environmental, elemental, planetary, and temporal systems.

The framework proposes that human psychological states emerge within nested systems rather than in isolation.

## Core Principle

Human beings are composed of elemental matter continuous with Earth, planetary systems, and stellar processes. Psychological life therefore exists within a broader material, environmental, and cosmological context.

## Research Aim

To explore whether measurable environmental, elemental, atmospheric, planetary, and temporal variables correlate with individual and collective psychological states.
