# Foundational Definition

Planetary Psychology™ is the study of the dynamic relationship between human consciousness, collective behavior, environmental systems, elemental composition, planetary conditions, and temporal cycles.

It proposes that human psychology is not isolated inside the individual but emerges from nested systems:

Individual → Body → Society → Environment → Planet → Cosmos

The strongest research version of Planetary Psychology™ does not claim that planets determine human behavior. Instead, it asks whether measurable planetary, environmental, elemental, and temporal variables correlate with psychological and behavioral patterns.
